Leave Policy

  1. An employee will be entitled to 12 Holidays a year.
  2. 3 national holidays (Jan 26th, August 15th and Oct 2nd) and rest 9 days decided by the company.
  3. Leaves taken, if uninformed, will be calculated first as paid leave (if the employee has got leave quota left for the quarter/year) or as unpaid leave.
  4. An employee can decide how does he/she want his/her leave to be processed as.
  5. All leaves unpaid and paid needs prior approval from the manager.
